# The N* Wager — Standing Public Disconfirmation Bounty

**Record:** `CL-2026-0001` (cohort: WAGER — excluded from all calibration curves)

## The wager

Realized human-supervisor-to-concurrent-AI-case ratios in production CX deployments
will center near **N\* ≈ 5 (range 3–8)**, not the ~20 vendors assume, under realistic
intervention rates. Mechanism: queueing behavior of interrupt-driven supervision —
at realistic intervention rates and switching costs, supervisor attention saturates
far below marketing ratios. Parameters (α, m₀, intervention rate) are **assumed, not
calibrated** — stated plainly, because the wager's credibility depends on parameter
honesty.

## Why this is a wager, not a forecast

No public referee exists: production supervision ratios are private operational
data. A claim without an external referee cannot enter a scored cohort — so this
one is registered as a wager, resolved by the bounty below, and excluded from every
calibration curve. Where it appears, it is described as a wager.

## The bounty (the resolution mechanism)

We will publicly retire this wager as **REFUTED** — prominently, in the register and
in the next issue — upon documentation of:

> **≥3 distinct production deployments sustaining >12 concurrent AI cases per human
> supervisor at stable quality for a full quarter.**

Qualifying documentation means, per deployment: (a) a named, verifiable deployment
(vendor + operator, or operator alone); (b) the sustained concurrency ratio and its
measurement window; (c) a quality series over the same window (CSAT/QA/resolution
integrity — no material degradation); (d) methodology inspectable enough to rule out
narrow task-scoping that drives intervention rates toward zero — the known escape
hatch, stated here in advance.

**Bounty consideration:** public resolution — the wager is retired as **REFUTED** in
this register and announced prominently in the next issue, with credit to the
submitter(s) by name (or anonymously on request). No monetary bounty is attached at
registration; if one is attached later, it will appear as a dated amendment to these
terms.

Submissions: via the register repository (public issue) or the contact address in
the repository README. Good-faith partial evidence (1–2 deployments) is acknowledged
in the wager's amendment block as it accumulates.

## What resolution means either way

Refutation would be *informative, not embarrassing*: it would bound the intervention
rate and task-scope conditions under which high-ratio supervision is real — exactly
the parameter the model needs. That conversion of a loss into instrumentation is the
house style.
